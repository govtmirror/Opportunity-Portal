Your journey to change the world starts here

Peace corps provides you with the opportunity to participate in many community service projects all around the world. Past volunteers went to Kenya, Vanuatu, and Lesotho, working on projects related to environment, business, and education.

Initially, all these opportunities may seem overwhelming, but this website is here to help you explore the possibilities.

Are you ready to commit?

The traditional Peace Corps program spans 27 months, with a variety of job assignments in over 70 countries. Most Volunteer placements are in this program.

In addition, a special program, Peace Corps Response, is open to seasoned professionals who have at least 10 years of proven work experience, and/or are past Peace Corps Volunteers who have successfully completed their service. Response assignments are highly technical, and are shorter term placements—three months to one year—in specific countries.

Be ready to discover yourself and learn from others

Be sure that service will improve the lives of people in communities around the world, inform the global perspectives of Americans, and be a life-defining experience for you.

You will receive practical benefits, learn new languages, gain leadership and international development experience, and work with your hosts as a partner and a friend.
